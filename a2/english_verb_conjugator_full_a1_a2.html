CEFR: A2-B1

S2: English Verb Tenses and Conjugations

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

1) English verbs use different tenses to show time and aspect.

英語の動詞は、時制とアスペクト（動作の状態）を表すためにさまざまな形をとります。

2) We combine auxiliary verbs (be, have, will, do) with the main verb.

助動詞（be, have, will, do など）と動詞の原形・ing形・過去分詞を組み合わせて時制を作ります。

3) This ficha is a global reference for main tenses in A1–A2.

このフィチャは、A1〜A2レベルでよく使う主な時制をまとめた総合表です。

📘 2. Global tense table（時制の総合表）

主な時制の形・例文・日本語訳の一覧です。

Tense Form Affirmative Negative (full / contracted) Question Example + JP
PRESENT SIMPLE（現在形）
Present Simple Subject + base / base+s I work. / She works. full: I do not work. / She does not work.
short: I don't work. / She doesn't work.		 Do you work? / Does she work? I work in a shop.
私は店で働いています。
PRESENT CONTINUOUS（現在進行形）
Present Continuous Subject + am/is/are + V-ing I am working. / She is working.
I'm working. / She's working.		 full: I am not working. / She is not working.
short: I'm not working. / She isn't working.		 Are you working? / Is she working? She is studying now.
彼女はいま勉強しています。
PAST SIMPLE（過去形）
Past Simple Subject + V-ed / irregular I worked. / She went. full: I did not work. / She did not go.
short: I didn't work. / She didn't go.		 Did you work? / Did she go? We watched a movie yesterday.
私たちは昨日映画を見ました。
PAST CONTINUOUS（過去進行形）
Past Continuous Subject + was/were + V-ing I was working. / They were working. full: I was not working. / They were not working.
short: I wasn't working. / They weren't working.		 Were you working? / Was he working? He was reading at 8 p.m.
彼は午後8時に読書をしていました。
PRESENT PERFECT（現在完了）
Present Perfect Subject + have/has + past participle I have worked. / She has worked.
I've worked. / She's worked.		 full: I have not worked. / She has not worked.
short: I haven't worked. / She hasn't worked.		 Have you worked? / Has she worked? I have visited Tokyo twice.
私は東京を2回訪れたことがあります。
PRESENT PERFECT CONTINUOUS（現在完了進行形）
Present Perfect Continuous Subject + have/has been + V-ing I have been studying.
I've been studying.		 full: I have not been studying.
short: I haven't been studying.		 Have you been studying? She has been working all day.
彼女は一日中働き続けています。
PAST PERFECT（過去完了）
Past Perfect Subject + had + past participle I had worked.
I'd worked.		 full: I had not worked.
short: I hadn't worked.		 Had you worked? They had left before 8 o'clock.
彼らは8時前にすでに出発していました。
FUTURE WITH WILL（単純未来）
Future (will) Subject + will + base I will work.
I'll work.		 full: I will not work.
short: I won't work.		 Will you work? She will call you tomorrow.
彼女は明日あなたに電話するでしょう。
FUTURE WITH GOING TO（～するつもり）
Future (going to) Subject + am/is/are going to + base I am going to study.
I'm going to study.		 full: I am not going to study.
short: I'm not going to study.		 Are you going to study? We are going to visit Kyoto.
私たちは京都を訪れる予定です。
FUTURE CONTINUOUS（未来進行形）
Future Continuous Subject + will be + V-ing I will be working.
I'll be working.		 full: I will not be working.
short: I won't be working.		 Will you be working? He will be studying at 9 p.m.
彼は午後9時には勉強しているでしょう。
FUTURE PERFECT（未来完了）
Future Perfect Subject + will have + past participle I will have finished.
I'll have finished.		 full: I will not have finished.
short: I won't have finished.		 Will you have finished? They will have arrived by noon.
彼らは正午までには到着しているでしょう。
IMPERATIVES（命令形）
Imperative base (no subject) Open the door. Don't open the door. — Be careful, please.
気をつけてください。
PASSIVE VOICE（受動態）
Passive (present) am/is/are + past participle The book is read. full: The book is not read.
short: The book isn't read.		 Is the book read? The room is cleaned every day.
その部屋は毎日掃除されます。
Passive (past) was/were + past participle The book was read. full: The book was not read.
short: The book wasn't read.		 Was the book read? The cake was made by my mother.
そのケーキは母によって作られました。
MODALS（助動詞）
Can Subject + can + base I can swim. full: I cannot swim.
short: I can't swim.		 Can you swim? She can speak English.
彼女は英語を話すことができます。
Must Subject + must + base I must study. full: I must not be late.
short: I mustn't be late.		 Must I study? You must wear a helmet.
ヘルメットを着用しなければなりません。

✏️ 3. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

Present simple: I get up at seven every day.
私は毎日7時に起きます。

Present continuous: She is eating breakfast now.
彼女はいま朝ごはんを食べています。

Past simple: They visited Osaka last week.
彼らは先週大阪を訪れました。

Present perfect: I have just finished my homework.
私はちょうど宿題を終えたところです。

Future (going to): We are going to travel in summer.
私たちは夏に旅行に行く予定です。

Passive: This song is loved by many people.
この歌は多くの人に愛されています。

Past continuous: They were waiting outside when it started to rain.
雨が降り始めたとき、彼らは外で待っていました。

📝 4. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

I am studying English now, but I usually study in the evening and I work in the morning.
私は今英語を勉強していますが、普段は夕方に勉強して、午前中は働いています。

Yesterday I went to the library, read a book, and wrote some notes about verb tenses.
昨日私は図書館へ行き、本を読み、動詞の時制についてメモを書きました。

I have been learning English for two years, and I will keep studying next year.
私は2年間英語を学んでいて、来年も勉強を続けるつもりです。

The movie was made in 2020 and it is watched by many students in our school.
その映画は2020年に作られ、私たちの学校の多くの生徒に見られています。

When I arrived at the station, the train had already left, so I had to wait for the next one.
駅に着いたとき、電車はすでに出発していたので、次の電車を待たなければなりませんでした。

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

A day with many verb tenses

Every morning, I get up at six and make breakfast for my family. While we are eating, my father is reading the news on his phone. Yesterday, we had a busy day, so we went to bed very early. I have studied English for a long time, and I have learned many verb forms. Next year, I am going to take an English test, and I will study even harder. Our house was built ten years ago, and it is cleaned every weekend. I can see that English verbs are not easy, but I must practice every day.

さまざまな動詞の形が出てくる一日

毎朝、私は6時に起きて家族のために朝ごはんを作ります。私たちが食べている間、父はスマホでニュースを読んでいます。昨日は忙しい一日だったので、とても早く寝ました。私は長い間英語を勉強していて、多くの動詞の形を学んできました。来年、私は英語のテストを受ける予定で、もっと一生懸命勉強するつもりです。私たちの家は10年前に建てられ、毎週末に掃除されています。英語の動詞は簡単ではありませんが、私は毎日練習しなければならないと感じています。

❓ 6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

1. What time does the narrator get up?
語り手は何時に起きますか。

At six.

2. What is the father doing while they are eating?
家族が食事をしている間、父は何をしていますか。

He is reading the news on his phone.

3. How long has the narrator studied English?
語り手はどのくらい英語を勉強していますか。

For a long time.

4. What is the narrator going to do next year?
語り手は来年何をする予定ですか。

Take an English test.

5. When was the house built?
その家はいつ建てられましたか。

It was built ten years ago.

🎯 Summary（まとめ）

  • English uses many verb tenses to show time and aspect.

    英語では多くの時制を使って、時間や動作の状態を表します。

  • Auxiliary verbs (be, have, will, do) combine with the main verb.

    be, have, will, do などの助動詞と動詞を組み合わせて形を作ります。

  • Each tense has affirmative, negative and question forms, often with contractions.

    それぞれの時制には、肯定文・否定文・疑問文があり、短縮形もよく使われます。

  • Practice with a global table helps you see the whole system and use verbs more confidently.

    このような総合表で練習すると、動詞の全体像が見え、自信を持って使えるようになります。
