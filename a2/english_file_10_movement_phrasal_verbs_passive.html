CEFR: A2-B1

Lesson 10: Movement, Phrasal Verbs & Passive

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

1. Expressing Movement（動きの表現）

We use movement verbs with prepositions/adverbs to show direction. → 動きの方向を表す。

  • Walk across the street / along the river / over the bridge.
  • Come in / go out / drive into / run out of.
  • We flew over the mountains.

2. Word Order of Phrasal Verbs（句動詞の語順）

A phrasal verb = verb + particle. → 動詞＋前置詞/副詞。

  • Turn off the TV / Turn the TV off / Turn it off.
  • Separable: put it on. Inseparable: look after her.

3. Passive Voice（受動態）

be + past participle → 「〜される」

  • Present: Cars are made in Japan.
  • Past: The Mona Lisa was painted by da Vinci.
  • Use passive when the doer is unknown or unimportant.

✏️ 2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • He walked across the street.
    • 通りを横切った。
  • Come in!
    • 入って。
  • She turned off the light.
    • 電気を消した。
  • Put it on.
    • 着て。
  • English is spoken in many countries.
    • 多くの国で話されている。
  • The car was stolen last night.
    • 昨晩盗まれた。
  • The letter was delivered this morning.
    • 手紙が今朝届けられました。

📝 3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • We walked along the river and across the bridge to reach the museum.
    • 川沿いを歩き、橋を渡って博物館に行った。
  • He looked for his keys everywhere, but he couldn’t find them.
    • 鍵をあちこち探したが見つからなかった。
  • My phone was stolen while I was on the train.
    • 電車の中で携帯を盗まれた。
  • These books were written by famous authors in the 19th century.
    • これらの本は19世紀に有名な作家によって書かれた。
  • She put on her coat and went out into the cold night.
    • コートを着て寒い夜へ出かけた。

🧩 4. Practice Exercises（練習問題）

Exercise A – Choose the correct option

1. We ran up the beach and jumped ______ the water. (into / out of)
into
2. Turn ______ the TV. (off / of)
off
3. CDs ______ used very much nowadays. (aren’t / weren’t)
aren’t
4. He walked ______ the café and ordered lunch. (into / out of)
into
5. The Mona Lisa ______ by Leonardo da Vinci. (was painted / painted)
was painted

Exercise B – Complete the sentence

1. She ______ her coat and walked out into the rain. (put)
put on
2. This bridge ______ over 100 years ago. (build)
was built
3. We walked ______ the park and sat near the fountain. (cross)
across
4. My bag ______ while I was on the train. (steal)
was stolen
5. Please ______ the music. I'm trying to sleep. (turn)
turn off

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

A Walk in the City

Yesterday I went for a walk in the city. I walked along the river and then across the bridge. I saw a man looking for his dog. He shouted "Come back!", but the dog ran out of the park and across the road. Later, I went into a café and sat down. I took off my coat and looked for my wallet. It wasn’t there. I think it was stolen. I called the police and filled in a report. They said the same thing happened last week. I hope my wallet is found soon.

街での散歩

昨日、街を散歩しました。川沿いを歩き、橋を渡りました。犬を探している男性がいて、「戻って！」と叫びましたが、犬は公園から飛び出して道路を横切りました。その後、カフェに入り座りました。コートを脱いで財布を探しましたが、ありませんでした。盗まれたと思います。警察に電話して書類を書きました。同じことが先週もあったと言われました。財布が見つかるといいのですが。

❓ 6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

1. Where did the writer walk?

筆者はどこを歩きましたか？

Along the river and across the bridge.
2. What happened to the dog?

犬はどうなりましたか？

It ran out of the park and across the road.
3. What was missing in the café?

カフェでなくなっていたものは何ですか？

The writer’s wallet.
4. What did the writer do after noticing the wallet was gone?

財布がないと気づいた後、筆者は何をしましたか？

Called the police and filled in a report.
5. Has this happened before?

これは以前にも起きましたか？

Yes, the police said it happened last week too.

🎯 7. Summary（まとめ）

  • Movement: across / along / over / into / out of.
    • 動きを表す前置詞：across / along / over / into / out of。
  • Phrasal verbs: separable vs inseparable.
    • 句動詞：分離可能（turn it off）と分離不可能（look after）。
  • Passive: be + past participle
    • 受動態：「〜される」。
  • Use passive when the doer is unknown or unimportant.
    • 行為者が不明または重要でないときに受動態を使う。
