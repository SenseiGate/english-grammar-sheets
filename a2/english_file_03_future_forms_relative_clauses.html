CEFR: A2-B1

Lesson 3: Future Forms & Relative Clauses

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

1) be going to (future plans & predictions)

We use be going to + verb to talk about future plans and predictions.

未来の計画や予想に使います。

PositiveNegativeQuestion
I’m going to study.I’m not going to study.Are you going to study?
She’s going to travel.She isn’t going to travel.Is she going to travel?

2) Present Continuous (future arrangements)

We use the present continuous for future arrangements with a fixed time or place.

決まった予定（時間・場所あり）に使います。

PositiveNegativeQuestion
I’m meeting Ken tomorrow.I’m not meeting Ken tomorrow.Are you meeting Ken tomorrow?

3) Relative Clauses (who / which / where)

Use who for people, which for things, and where for places.

who＝人、which＝物、where＝場所

  • A teacher is a person who helps students.
  • A camera is a thing which takes photos.
  • A park is a place where people relax.

✏️ 2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • I’m going to buy a new notebook.
    • 新しいノートを買うつもりです。
  • She’s meeting her friend tonight.
    • 彼女は今夜友達に会います。
  • It’s going to snow later.
    • 後で雪が降りそうです。
  • We’re having lunch at 1 p.m.
    • 午後1時に昼食の予定です。
  • That’s the shop where I get my coffee.
    • そこは私がコーヒーを買う店です。
  • He’s the man who fixed my bike.
    • 彼は自転車を直してくれた人です。
  • This is the pen which writes very smoothly.
    • これはとても書きやすいペンです。

📝 3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • I’m going to start a new project next week because I finally have time.
    • 時間ができたので来週新しいプロジェクトを始めるつもりです。
  • We’re meeting our coworkers at the station tomorrow morning.
    • 明日の朝、駅で同僚と会う予定です。
  • Look at those clouds—they’re going to bring heavy rain.
    • あの雲は大雨になりそうです。
  • That’s the café where I usually study on weekends.
    • そこは週末によく勉強するカフェです。
  • She’s the person who always helps new students feel comfortable.
    • 彼女は新入生を安心させてくれる人です。

🧩 4. Practice Exercises（練習問題）

Exercise A – Choose the correct option

2. They’re going to (travel / traveling) next month.

3. Look! That dog is going to (jump / jumping).

4. She (isn’t going to come / doesn’t come) to the event.
isn’t going to come

are going to

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

A Busy Weekend Ahead

This weekend is going to be very busy for me. On Saturday morning, I’m meeting a friend who lives in another city. We’re having breakfast at a café which opened last month. After that, I’m going to buy some tools because I want to fix a shelf in my apartment. In the afternoon, I’m visiting a park where many people go to relax. I’m going to take photos because the trees look beautiful in winter.

On Sunday, I’m going to study at home. I have an online meeting with a teacher who helps me with English writing. Later, I’m cooking dinner for my family. It’s going to be a simple meal, but I hope everyone enjoys it. Even though the weekend is full, I feel excited about everything I’m going to do.

忙しくなりそうな週末

今週末はとても忙しくなりそうです。土曜の朝、別の町に住んでいる友達と会う予定です。私たちは先月オープンしたカフェで朝食を食べます。その後、アパートの棚を直したいので工具を買うつもりです。午後は、多くの人がリラックスしに行く公園を訪れます。冬の木々がきれいなので写真を撮るつもりです。

日曜日は家で勉強します。英語ライティングを手伝ってくれる先生とオンラインミーティングがあります。その後、家族のために夕食を作ります。簡単な料理ですが、みんなが楽しんでくれたら嬉しいです。忙しい週末ですが、どれも楽しみです。

❓ 6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

1. Who is the writer meeting on Saturday morning?

書き手は土曜の朝に誰と会いますか？

A friend who lives in another city.
2) When did the café open?
そのカフェはいつオープンしましたか？
Last month.
3. Why is the writer going to buy tools?

なぜ工具を買うつもりですか？

To fix a shelf in the apartment.
4. What is the writer doing on Sunday afternoon?

日曜の午後は何をしますか？

Cooking dinner for the family.
5. Which person helps the writer with English writing?

英語ライティングを手伝ってくれるのは誰ですか？

A teacher.

🎯 Summary（まとめ）

  • be going to = future plans & predictions
    • 未来の計画・予想。
  • Present continuous = fixed arrangements
    • 決まった予定。
  • who / which / where = explain people, things, places
    • 人・物・場所を説明する語。
  • be going to + evidence = prediction; present continuous = confirmed arrangement.
    • 証拠あり→going to、確定した予定→現在進行形。
  • who / which / where never change form in the clause.
    • 関係代名詞の形は変わらない。
