CEFR: A2-B1

Lesson 9: Conditionals & Present Perfect

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

1. Second Conditional（仮定法過去）

We use if + past and would / wouldn’t + verb to talk about imaginary or unlikely situations. → 現実ではない仮定や可能性の低い未来について話すときに使います。

  • If I had more time, I’d travel more.
  • Would you go if it snowed?
  • If we went by car, we could stop on the way.

2. Present Perfect + for / since（現在完了）

We use have / has + past participle to talk about actions that started in the past and continue now. → 過去に始まり、今も続いていることを表します。

  • I’ve lived here for 10 years. / She’s worked here since 2015.
  • Use for + period, since + starting point.

3. Present Perfect vs Past Simple（現在完了と過去形の違い）

Use past simple for finished time. Use present perfect for unfinished time or experience. → 完了した過去は過去形、今も続く場合は現在完了。

  • I lived in Tokyo for 2 years. → now finished
  • I’ve lived in Tokyo since 2020. → still true

✏️ 2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • If I won the lottery, I’d travel the world.
    • 宝くじに当たったら、世界を旅します。
  • Would you go if it snowed?
    • 雪が降ったら行きますか？
  • I’ve lived here since 2010.
    • 2010年からここに住んでいます。
  • She’s worked here for five years.
    • 彼女はここで5年間働いています。
  • I met him last year.
    • 去年彼に会いました。
  • I’ve met him before.
    • 以前彼に会ったことがあります。
  • If I were taller, I'd play basketball.
    • もっと背が高ければ、バスケットボールをするのに。

📝 3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • If I didn’t have to work on weekends, I’d visit my family more often.
    • 週末に働かなくてよければ、もっと頻繁に家族に会いに行きます。
  • We’d get there faster if we took a taxi.
    • タクシーに乗れば、もっと早く着きます。
  • I’ve lived in this town for over ten years, and I still love it.
    • この町に10年以上住んでいますが、今でも大好きです。
  • She’s had her cat since she was a child.
    • 彼女は子供の頃から猫を飼っています。
  • I went to London in 2018, but I haven’t been back since.
    • 2018年にロンドンに行きましたが、それ以来行っていません。

🧩 4. Practice Exercises（練習問題）

Exercise A – Choose the correct option

1. If I had more money, I ______ travel more. (will / would)
would
2. How long ______ you lived here? (have / did)
have
3. I ______ in Osaka for three years. (lived / have lived)
have lived
4. If it rained, we ______ go to the beach. (won’t / wouldn’t)
wouldn’t
5. She ______ her phone yesterday. (lost / has lost)
lost

Exercise B – Complete the sentence

1. If I ______ more free time, I'd learn to paint. (have)
had
2. She ______ in this city since she was a child. (live)
has lived
3. I went to Osaka last year, but I ______ there since. (not / go)
haven't been
4. We ______ here for ten years next month. (live)
will have lived
5. If she ______ harder, she'd get better results. (study)
studied

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

My Cousin’s Life in Canada

My cousin lives in Canada. He’s lived there since 2010 and he really enjoys it. He works as a designer and has had the same job for over ten years. If I lived in Canada, I think I’d enjoy the nature and the quiet life. I’ve visited him twice, but I haven’t been there recently. If I had more vacation time, I’d go again this year. He says the winters are very cold, but the summers are beautiful. He’s had a dog since 2015, and they go hiking together every weekend.

いとこのカナダでの生活

いとこはカナダに住んでいます。2010年からそこに住んでいて、とても気に入っています。彼はデザイナーとして働いていて、10年以上同じ仕事を続けています。もし私がカナダに住んでいたら、自然や静かな生活を楽しめると思います。私は彼のところに2回行ったことがありますが、最近は行っていません。もっと休みがあれば、今年また行くのにと思います。彼によると、冬はとても寒いですが、夏はとてもきれいだそうです。2015年から犬を飼っていて、毎週末いっしょにハイキングに行きます。

❓ 6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

1. How long has the cousin lived in Canada?

いとこはどれくらいカナダに住んでいますか？

Since 2010.
2. What does the cousin do for work?

いとこはどんな仕事をしていますか？

He works as a designer.
3. How many times has the writer visited the cousin?

筆者はいとこを何回訪れましたか？

Twice.
4. What would the writer do if they had more vacation time?

もっと休みがあれば、筆者は何をしますか？

They’d go to Canada again this year.
5. Since when has the cousin had a dog?

いとこはいつから犬を飼っていますか？

Since 2015.

🎯 7. Summary（まとめ）

  • Second conditional: if + past → would / wouldn’t + verb
    • 仮定・非現実の状況。
  • Present perfect + for / since: ongoing actions
    • 過去に始まり今も続くこと。
  • Past simple: finished past
    • 完全に終わった過去の出来事。
  • Use for + period, since + starting point.
    • for は期間、since は開始時点。
