CEFR: A2-B1

Lesson 6: Future Forms – Predictions

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

1. will / won’t for predictions（予測）

We use will / won’t + verb to talk about the future when we guess or believe something. → 予測や「〜と思う」ときに使います。

  • I think it’ll rain tomorrow. → 明日雨が降ると思います。
  • She won’t pass the test. → 彼女はテストに合格しないでしょう。

2. will / won’t / shall for decisions, offers, promises（決定・申し出・約束）

Use will / won’t for quick decisions, offers, and promises. Use shall I / shall we to offer help or suggestions. → 即決・申し出・約束、「〜しましょうか」に使います。

  • I’ll help you. / I won’t tell anyone. / Shall I open the window?

3. Future forms review（未来形のまとめ）

FormExampleUse
will / won’tI’ll call you later.prediction / decision / offer
be going toIt’s going to rain.visible prediction / plan
present continuousI’m meeting her tomorrow.fixed future plan

✏️ 2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • I think it’ll be sunny tomorrow.
    • 明日は晴れると思います。
  • I won’t go out tonight.
    • 今夜は出かけません。
  • She’ll love the gift.
    • 彼女はプレゼントを気に入るでしょう。
  • We’re meeting at 7 p.m.
    • 午後7時に会う予定です。
  • Look at the clouds! It’s going to rain.
    • 雲を見て！雨が降りそう。
  • I’ll help you with your homework.
    • 宿題を手伝います。
  • Shall we sit over there?
    • あそこで座りましょうか？

📝 3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • I’m sure you’ll enjoy the movie because it’s very funny and exciting.
    • とても面白くてワクワクするので、きっと映画を楽しめると思います。
  • We’re going to visit Kyoto next weekend because we love traditional culture.
    • 伝統文化が好きなので、来週末に京都を訪れる予定です。
  • Don’t worry about the test. I’ll help you study and you’ll do well.
    • テストのことは心配しないで。勉強を手伝うから、きっとうまくいきます。
  • She won’t come to the party because she has to work late tonight.
    • 今夜遅くまで仕事なので、彼女はパーティーに来ません。
  • Shall we try the new restaurant near the station this Friday?
    • 今週の金曜日、駅の近くの新しいレストランに行ってみませんか？

🧩 4. Practice Exercises（練習問題）

Exercise A – Choose the correct option

1. I think it ______ tomorrow. (will rain / rains)
will rain
2. Look at those clouds! It’s ______. (going to rain / will rain)
going to rain
3. I just decided to stay home tonight, so I ______ cook dinner. (will / am going to)
will
4. ______ I help you with that box? (Shall / Will)
Shall
5. She ______ come to the meeting. She’s sick. (won’t / doesn’t)
won’t

Exercise B – Complete the sentence

1. I’m sure you ____________________________ the exam. (pass)
will pass
2. We ____________________________ dinner with my parents tomorrow. (have)
are having / are going to have
3. It’s very cold. I think it ____________________________ tonight. (snow)
will snow / is going to snow
4. ____________________________ we watch a movie after dinner? (shall)
Shall
5. I ____________________________ you as soon as I arrive. (call)
will call

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

Moving to a New City for Work

Next month, my company is going to open a new office in another city. My manager thinks it will be a great opportunity for me, so he has asked me to move there for one year. At first, I was nervous because I don’t know anyone in that city, but I’m sure I’ll make new friends. I’ll start looking for an apartment this weekend, and I’m going to visit the area next Saturday. My colleagues say the city is very safe and the food is delicious, so I think I’ll enjoy living there. I’ll miss my family, but we’ll have video calls every week. It will be a big change, but I believe it will be a positive one.

仕事で新しい街へ引っ越すことに

来月、私の会社は別の都市に新しいオフィスを開く予定です。上司はそれが私にとって良いチャンスになると思い、1年間そこへ行くように頼みました。最初は、その街に知り合いがいないので不安でしたが、新しい友達ができると信じています。今週末にアパートを探し始め、来週の土曜日にその地域を見に行くつもりです。同僚によると、その街はとても安全で、食べ物もおいしいそうなので、そこでの生活を楽しめると思います。家族が恋しくなりますが、毎週ビデオ通話をするつもりです。大きな変化ですが、きっと良い変化になると信じています。

❓ 6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

1. Why is the writer moving to another city?

なぜ書き手は別の都市に引っ越すのですか？

Because the company is opening a new office and the manager asked them to go.
2. How long will the writer stay in the new city?

どのくらいその新しい街にいる予定ですか？

For one year.
3. What are the writer’s colleagues saying about the city?

同僚たちはその街について何と言っていますか？

That it is very safe and the food is delicious.
4. How will the writer keep in touch with their family?

家族とはどのように連絡を取り続けますか？

By having video calls every week.
5. Does the writer think the change will be positive?

この変化は良いものになると思っていますか？

Yes, they believe it will be a positive change.

🎯 7. Summary（まとめ）

  • will / won’t for predictions and quick decisions.
    • 予測・即決に使う。
  • shall I / shall we for offers and suggestions.
    • 申し出・提案に使う。
  • be going to for plans and visible predictions.
    • 計画・見てわかる予測。
  • present continuous for fixed future plans.
    • 予定された未来に使う。
