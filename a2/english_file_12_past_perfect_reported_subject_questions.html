CEFR: A2-B1

Lesson 12: Past Perfect, Reported Speech & Subject Questions

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

1. Past Perfect（過去完了）

We use had + past participle to talk about an earlier past action. → 過去のある時点より前に起こったこと。

  • When I woke up, it had snowed.
  • She realized she had forgotten her book.
  • We got home – the match hadn’t started.

2. Reported Speech（間接話法）

We use said / told + (that) + changed tense to report what someone said.

  • ‘I love you.’ → He said (that) he loved me.
  • ‘I’ve just arrived.’ → She said (that) she had just arrived.
  • ‘We’ll come at eight.’ → He told me (that) they would come at eight.

3. Subject Questions（主語の疑問文）

When the question word is the subject, we don’t use do/does/did.

  • Who painted The Milkmaid?
  • Which actor won the Oscar?
  • How many people live near the school?

✏️ 2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • I’d left my wallet at home.
    • 財布を家に忘れてきた。
  • He said he was tired.
    • 彼は疲れていると言った。
  • Who wrote this book?
    • この本を書いたのは誰？
  • She hadn’t finished her homework.
    • 宿題を終えていなかった。
  • They told me they would come.
    • 来ると言われた。
  • Who invented the telephone?
    • 電話を発明したのは誰ですか？
  • By the time we arrived, the meeting had already started.
    • 私たちが到着したときには、会議はすでに始まっていました。

📝 3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • When I got to the station, I realized I had left my phone at home.
    • 駅に着いたとき、家に携帯を忘れたことに気づきました。
  • She said that she had just arrived and that she was very tired.
    • 彼女は着いたばかりで、とても疲れていると言いました。
  • The person who discovered penicillin changed the history of medicine forever.
    • ペニシリンを発見した人は、医学の歴史を永遠に変えました。
  • He told me that he didn’t want to go to the party.
    • 彼はパーティーに行きたくないと言いました。
  • We couldn’t get in because we had lost the keys.
    • 鍵をなくしたので中に入れませんでした。

🧩 4. Practice Exercises（練習問題）

Exercise A – Choose the correct form

1. I couldn’t get in because I ______ my keys. (lost / had lost)
had lost
2. She said she ______ a new phone. (buys / had bought)
had bought
3. Who ______ this painting? (painted / did paint)
painted
4. He told me he ______ tired. (was / is)
was
5. When I got home, I saw someone ______ the window. (broke / had broken)
had broken

Exercise B – Complete the sentence

1. When she arrived, the party ______ already ______. (finish)
had already finished
2. He told me he ______ the report the day before. (send)
had sent
3. ______ won the last World Cup? (who)
Who
4. She said she ______ to call me later. (going)
was going
5. By the time I got to the station, the train ______. (already / leave)
had already left

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

A Strange Discovery at Home

Yesterday, I got home and saw that the door was open. I was surprised because I had locked it. I went inside and noticed that someone had moved my books. I called my friend and told him what had happened. He said he would come over. When he arrived, he asked, “Who opened the door?” I said I didn’t know. We checked the windows, but they hadn’t been broken. It was very strange.

家で起きた不思議な出来事

昨日、家に帰るとドアが開いていました。鍵をかけたはずなので驚きました。中に入ると、本が動かされていることに気づきました。友達に電話して、何が起こったかを伝えました。彼は「すぐ行くよ」と言いました。到着すると、「誰がドアを開けたの？」と聞かれました。私は「わからない」と答えました。窓を確認しましたが、壊されていませんでした。不思議な出来事でした。

❓ 6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

1. Why was the writer surprised?

なぜ筆者は驚いたのですか？

Because the door was open even though they had locked it.
2. What had someone moved?

誰かが動かしたものは何ですか？

The writer’s books.
3. What did the friend say he would do?

友達は何をすると言いましたか？

He said he would come over.
4. What did they check after the friend arrived?

友達が到着した後、何を確認しましたか？

They checked the windows.
5. Were the windows broken?

窓は壊されていましたか？

No, they hadn’t been broken.

🎯 7. Summary（まとめ）

  • Past Perfect: had + past participle
    • 過去のある時点より前の出来事。
  • Reported Speech: said / told + that + tense shift
    • love → loved, will → would, have done → had done。
  • Subject Questions: No do/does/did when the WH-word is the subject.
    • 疑問詞が主語のときは do/does/did を使わない。
