Lesson 2: Present Perfect Continuous

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

1. Meaning and Focus（意味と焦点）

The Present Perfect Continuous describes an activity that began in the past and continues into the present, or one that has recently stopped but still affects the current situation. → 過去に始まり今も続いている、または最近まで続いていて今に影響がある動作を表す。

UseExample
Ongoing activityShe has been studying all morning.
Recent activity with present effectHe has been running, so he’s out of breath.

2. Form and Structure（形と文型）

The structure is have/has + been + -ing. We use contractions naturally in spoken English. → have/has + been + 動詞-ing の形。会話では短縮形がよく使われる。

FormExample
AffirmativeShe has been reading.
NegativeThey haven’t been working.
QuestionHave you been waiting long?

3. Duration and Evidence（期間と証拠）

We often use for and since to show duration, and we use this tense when we see physical or emotional evidence of an activity. → for / since で期間を示し、動作の「証拠」があるときにも使う。

  • She looks tired because she has been working late. → 遅くまで働いていたので疲れて見える。
  • The ground is wet because it has been raining. → 雨が降っていたので地面が濡れている。

✏️ 2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • I have been reviewing grammar points all afternoon.
    • 午後ずっと文法を復習しています。
  • She has been living abroad since last year.
    • 彼女は去年から海外に住んでいます。
  • They have been discussing the proposal for hours.
    • 何時間も提案について議論しています。
  • He has been fixing his bike, so his hands are dirty.
    • 自転車を直していたので手が汚れています。
  • We have been planning a trip for weeks.
    • 数週間ずっと旅行の計画をしています。
  • The students have been practicing for the competition.
    • 生徒たちは大会のために練習しています。
  • My phone has been acting strange lately.
    • 最近スマホの調子が変です。

📝 3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • I have been preparing a presentation for tomorrow’s meeting, and I finally feel confident about the content.
    • 明日の会議のプレゼンを準備していて、やっと内容に自信が持てました。
  • She has been researching different universities because she wants to study abroad next year.
    • 来年留学したいので、彼女はいろいろな大学を調べています。
  • They have been renovating their apartment for months, and the place is starting to look completely different.
    • 何か月もアパートを改装していて、部屋がすっかり変わってきました。
  • He has been practicing his speech repeatedly, hoping to improve his pronunciation and rhythm.
    • 発音とリズムを良くしたいので、彼はスピーチを何度も練習しています。
  • We have been analyzing customer feedback, and several important patterns have started to appear.
    • 顧客のフィードバックを分析していて、いくつか重要な傾向が見えてきました。

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

Improving My Work–Life Balance

Over the past few months, I have been trying to improve my work–life balance. For years, I focused almost entirely on my job, often staying late at the office and skipping breaks. This routine left me exhausted, and I realized that I had been ignoring my health. Recently, I have been setting clearer boundaries and organizing my schedule more carefully. I have been taking short breaks during the day, and I have been exercising three times a week. These changes have helped me feel more energetic and focused.

Another habit I have been developing is keeping a daily journal. I write about what I have been learning, what challenges I have been facing, and what goals I want to achieve. Reflecting on my day has been helping me understand my emotions better. Although I still have busy weeks, I feel that my life has been becoming more balanced and meaningful. Small adjustments have been creating a surprisingly positive impact.

ワークライフバランスの改善

ここ数か月、私は仕事と生活のバランスを改善しようとしています。何年も仕事中心の生活を続け、残業したり休憩を取らなかったりしていました。その結果、疲れ切ってしまい、自分の健康を無視していたことに気づきました。最近は境界線をはっきりさせ、スケジュールを丁寧に管理するようにしています。日中に短い休憩を取り、週に3回運動するようになりました。こうした変化のおかげで、以前より元気で集中できるようになりました。

もう一つの新しい習慣は、毎日の日記を書くことです。学んだこと、直面した課題、達成したい目標について書いています。1日を振り返ることで、自分の感情をより理解できるようになりました。忙しい週もありますが、生活がよりバランスの取れた、意味のあるものになってきたと感じています。小さな調整が驚くほど良い影響を生んでいます。

❓ 6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

1. Why did the writer feel exhausted in the past?
Because they focused too much on work and skipped breaks.
2. What new habits has the writer been practicing?
Taking breaks, exercising regularly, and keeping a daily journal.
3. How has journaling been helping the writer?
It has been helping them understand their emotions better.
4. What changes have improved the writer’s energy and focus?
Taking breaks and exercising regularly.
5. What overall effect have these adjustments been creating?
They have been making the writer’s life more balanced and meaningful.

🎯 7. Summary（まとめ）

  • Present Perfect Continuous = have/has + been + -ing.
    • have/has + been + 動詞-ing。
  • Used for ongoing actions and recent activities with present effects.
    • 続いている動作・今に影響がある動作。
  • Often appears with for / since / recently / lately.
    • for / since / recently / lately とよく使う。
  • Shows duration, evidence, and emotional/physical results.
    • 期間・証拠・結果を示す。
