CEFR: B1-B2

Lesson 3: Past Continuous + Past Simple

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

1. Past Continuous（過去進行形）

The Past Continuous (was/were + -ing) describes an action that was in progress at a specific moment in the past. It often provides background information or sets the scene in storytelling. → 過去のある時点で進行中だった動作。物語の背景描写にもよく使う。

UseExample
Background actionThe wind was blowing strongly.
Action in progressI was studying at 10 pm.

2. Past Simple（過去形）

The Past Simple (V2) expresses completed actions or events that happened at a definite time in the past. → 過去のある時点で完了した出来事。

UseExample
Completed actionThe phone rang suddenly.
Sequence of eventsHe opened the door and stepped inside.

3. Interaction of the Two Tenses（2つの時制の組み合わせ）

When these tenses appear together, the Past Continuous provides the background, while the Past Simple introduces a shorter action that interrupts it. → 過去進行形が背景、過去形が割り込みの動作。

  • I was cooking when the phone rang. → 電話が鳴ったとき、料理をしていた。
  • She was walking home when it started to rain. → 雨が降り始めたとき、歩いて帰っていた。

✏️ 2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • I was reviewing my notes when the announcement started.
    • アナウンスが始まったとき、ノートを見直していました。
  • They were discussing the plan when the manager entered.
    • マネージャーが入ってきたとき、計画を話し合っていました。
  • She was preparing dinner when the lights went out.
    • 停電したとき、夕食を準備していました。
  • The students were chatting while the teacher was writing on the board.
    • 先生が板書している間、生徒たちは話していました。
  • We were walking along the river when we saw a shooting star.
    • 川沿いを歩いていたとき、流れ星を見ました。
  • He dropped his keys while he was getting out of the car.
    • 車を降りているとき、鍵を落としました。
  • I was checking my email when the meeting reminder popped up.
    • メールを確認していたとき、会議のリマインダーが出ました。

📝 3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • I was walking through the station yesterday when I noticed a new café that had just opened.
    • 昨日駅を歩いていたとき、新しくオープンしたカフェに気づきました。
  • She was studying for her exam when she realized she had left her notes at school.
    • 試験勉強をしていたとき、ノートを学校に忘れたことに気づきました。
  • We were having a serious discussion when the fire alarm suddenly went off.
    • 真剣に話し合っていたとき、突然火災警報器が鳴りました。
  • They were driving through heavy rain when their GPS stopped working.
    • 大雨の中を運転していたとき、GPSが動かなくなりました。
  • I was taking a shower when I remembered an important appointment I had forgotten to confirm.
    • シャワーを浴びていたとき、確認し忘れていた大事な予定を思い出しました。

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

An Unexpected Evening on the Street

Last Friday evening, I was walking to the train station after work when something unusual caught my attention. The street was crowded, and many people were rushing home, but a small group had gathered near a bus stop. As I got closer, I noticed that everyone was looking at a man who was playing the violin. He was performing passionately, and the music echoed beautifully through the street. While I was listening, I suddenly heard a loud crash behind me. A cyclist had fallen because a pedestrian stepped into the bike lane without looking.

People immediately reacted. Some were helping the cyclist stand up, while others were checking if the pedestrian was injured. I was calling for assistance when the violinist stopped playing and came over to help as well. He explained that he had been performing there every Friday for months, and accidents happened often because the area was poorly lit. While we were talking, the cyclist recovered and thanked everyone for helping. The situation calmed down, and I continued my walk, thinking about how quickly an ordinary evening could change.

思いがけない金曜の夕方

先週の金曜日の夕方、仕事の帰りに駅へ歩いていたとき、少し変わったことが目に入りました。通りは混雑していて、多くの人が急いで帰宅していましたが、バス停の近くに小さな人だかりができていました。近づいてみると、みんながバイオリンを弾いている男性を見ていました。彼は情熱的に演奏していて、音楽が通りに美しく響いていました。聞いていると、突然後ろで大きな音がしました。歩行者が自転車レーンに飛び出し、自転車が転倒したのです。

人々はすぐに反応しました。何人かは自転車の人を助け起こし、別の人たちは歩行者が怪我をしていないか確認していました。私は助けを呼んでいると、バイオリン奏者も演奏を止めて手伝いに来ました。彼は毎週金曜日にここで演奏していて、この場所は照明が暗いため事故がよく起きると説明しました。話している間に、自転車の人は回復し、みんなに感謝しました。状況が落ち着いたので、私は散歩を続け、普通の夕方がこんなに急に変わるものだと考えました。

❓ 6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

1. What were people doing near the bus stop?
They were watching a man playing the violin.
2. Why did the cyclist fall?
A pedestrian stepped into the bike lane without looking.
3. What did the violinist explain?
He had been performing there every Friday and accidents happened often.
4. What was the writer doing during the accident?
They were calling for assistance.
5. How did the writer feel at the end?
They were thinking about how quickly an ordinary evening could change.

🎯 7. Summary（まとめ）

  • Past Continuous = background action.
    • 背景の動作。
  • Past Simple = interrupting action.
    • 割り込みの動作。
  • Use when for interruptions.
    • when は割り込み。
  • Use while for background.
    • while は背景。
  • Useful for storytelling and unexpected events.
    • 物語や予期しない出来事の描写に便利。
