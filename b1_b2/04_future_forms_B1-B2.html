CEFR: B1-B2

Lesson 4: Future Forms

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

1. Will（その場の決定・予想・確信）

Will expresses spontaneous decisions, predictions based on personal opinion, and strong beliefs about the future. → その場の決定・主観的な予想・強い確信を表す。

UseExample
Spontaneous decisionI’m cold. I’ll close the window.
Prediction (opinion-based)I think the meeting will finish late.
Promise / willingnessI’ll support you with the presentation.

2. Going to（計画・証拠のある予想・意図）

Be going to is used for intentions, structured plans, and predictions supported by visible evidence. → 意図・計画・根拠のある予想に使う。

UseExample
IntentionI’m going to start studying earlier this semester.
Planned actionWe’re going to visit Kyoto next month.
Evidence-based predictionLook at those clouds. It’s going to rain soon.

3. Present Continuous（確定した予定・調整済みの約束）

The Present Continuous (am/is/are + -ing) expresses fixed arrangements, especially when the time and place are already decided. → 時間・場所が決まっている確定した予定に使う。

UseExample
ArrangementI’m meeting my supervisor tomorrow morning.
Scheduled planShe’s flying to Osaka next week for a conference.

✏️ 2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • I’ll review your report this afternoon.
    • 今日の午後、レポートを確認します。
  • We’re going to upgrade our computer system soon.
    • もうすぐシステムをアップグレードする予定です。
  • She’s meeting her boss to discuss the new project.
    • 新しいプロジェクトについて上司と会います。
  • Look! That car is going to hit the wall!
    • 見て！あの車、壁にぶつかりそう！
  • I think he’ll need more time to finish.
    • 彼は終えるのにもっと時間が必要になりそうです。
  • They’re going to move to a bigger apartment next year.
    • 来年もっと広いアパートに引っ越す予定です。
  • I’ll call you once the meeting ends.
    • 会議が終わったら電話します。

📝 3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • I think it will be a demanding week because several departments are preparing major presentations.
    • 複数の部署が大きなプレゼンを準備しているので、忙しい週になりそうです。
  • We’re going to launch a new project next month, and the team has already started gathering data.
    • 来月新しいプロジェクトを開始する予定で、チームはすでにデータ収集を始めています。
  • She’s meeting her friend for dinner tonight, but she’ll probably return early because she has an exam tomorrow.
    • 今夜友達と夕食ですが、明日試験があるので早く帰るようです。
  • Look at those dark clouds! It’s going to rain heavily, so I’ll take a taxi instead of walking.
    • あの黒い雲を見て！強く雨が降りそうだから、歩かずにタクシーにします。
  • They’re flying to Canada next week, and they’re going to stay there for two months to complete their research.
    • 来週カナダへ飛び、研究を終えるために2か月滞在する予定です。

🧩 4. Practice Exercises（練習問題）

Exercise A – Choose the correct option

1. I think it (will / is going to) snow tomorrow.
will
2. Look at the sky! It (will / is going to) rain.
is going to
3. She (will / is meeting) her friend at 6 pm.
is meeting
4. We (will / are going to) buy a new sofa next month.
are going to
5. I’m thirsty. I (will / am going to) get some water.
will

Exercise B – Complete the sentence

1. She ________ (meet) her sister tomorrow because they booked the restaurant last week.
is meeting
2. I think he ________ (be) late.
will be
3. They ________ (move) to a new apartment next year.
are going to move
4. Look! The baby ________ (fall).
is going to fall
5. I’m hungry. I ________ (make) a sandwich.

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

A Busy Month of New Beginnings

Next month is going to be an important turning point for me because several aspects of my life are changing at once. I’m starting a new job in a different city, and I’m going to move into a smaller but more convenient apartment near the office. I’m meeting my new team next week, and they seem highly motivated and experienced. I think I’ll enjoy working with them, especially because the company is launching multiple long‑term projects this year.

I’m also going to buy new furniture, since my previous apartment was much larger and most of my old items won’t fit. Look at these boxes! I think I’ll need help carrying them, especially the heavier ones. My friend said he’ll come on Saturday, so we’re going to organize everything together. After that, I’m meeting another friend for dinner to celebrate the move and talk about my new job. It’s going to be a busy month, but I’m sure it will be a meaningful start to a new chapter in my life.

新しい生活が始まる忙しい月

来月は、生活のいくつかの面が同時に変わるため、私にとって大きな転機になりそうです。 新しい仕事が別の街で始まり、オフィスの近くにある小さめですが便利なアパートに引っ越す予定です。 来週新しいチームと会う予定で、みんなとても意欲的で経験豊富に見えます。 会社は今年いくつかの長期プロジェクトを開始するので、一緒に働くのが楽しみだと思います。

また、前のアパートは広かったため、新しい家具を買う予定です。 この箱を見てください！特に重いものは運ぶのに手伝いが必要になりそうです。 友達が土曜日に来てくれると言っていたので、一緒に整理する予定です。 その後、引っ越しと新しい仕事のお祝いとして、別の友達と夕食に行く予定です。 忙しい月になりそうですが、新しい生活の良いスタートになると確信しています。

❓ 6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

1. Why is next month important for the writer?
Because several major changes are happening at the same time.
2. Why does the writer need new furniture?
Because their old apartment was larger and their furniture won’t fit.
3. Who is helping the writer on Saturday?
A friend who offered to help carry the boxes.
4. What is the writer doing after organizing the apartment?
They are meeting another friend for dinner.
5. Why does the writer feel positive about the upcoming month?
Because it feels like a meaningful start to a new chapter in their life.

🎯 7. Summary（まとめ）

  • Will → spontaneous decisions, predictions, strong beliefs.
    • その場の決定・主観的予想。
  • Going to → plans, intentions, evidence-based predictions.
    • 計画・意図・根拠のある予想。
  • Present Continuous → fixed arrangements with set time/place.
    • 時間・場所が決まった予定。
  • Context determines the correct form.
    • 文脈が最も重要。
