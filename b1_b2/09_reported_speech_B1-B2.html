CEFR: B1-B2

Lesson 9: Reported Speech

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

1. Reported Statements（間接話法：文）

We use reported speech to tell someone what another person said. → 他の人が言ったことを伝えるときに使います。 Pronouns and time expressions often change. → 代名詞・時間表現が変わります。

Direct SpeechReported Speech
“I am tired.”He said he was tired.
“We will go tomorrow.”They said they would go the next day.

Backshifting（時制の一致）
Present → Past
Past → Past Perfect
Will → Would
→ 時制が一つ過去に下がることが多い。

2. Reported Questions（間接話法：疑問文）

We remove question order and use normal sentence order. → 語順は平叙文に戻します。 We use asked instead of said. → said ではなく asked を使います。

Direct QuestionReported Question
“Where do you live?”She asked where I lived.
“Did he call?”She asked if he had called.

3. Reported Commands & Requests（命令・依頼の間接話法）

Use told + object + to + verb for commands. → 命令は told + 人 + to + 動詞。 Use asked + object + to + verb for polite requests. → 依頼は asked + 人 + to + 動詞。

DirectReported
“Close the door!”He told me to close the door.
“Please help me.”She asked me to help her.

✏️ 2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • He said he was busy.
    • 彼は忙しいと言いました。
  • She told me to wait.
    • 彼女は私に待つように言いました。
  • They said they would call later.
    • 彼らは後で電話すると言いました。
  • She asked where I had gone.
    • 彼女は私がどこへ行ったのか尋ねました。
  • He asked me to send the file.
    • 彼はファイルを送るよう頼みました。
  • She said she had finished the report.
    • 彼女はレポートを終えたと言いました。
  • They asked if I could join the meeting.
    • 彼らは私が会議に参加できるか尋ねました。

📝 3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • He said that he had been working all night, so he needed a short break.
    • 彼は一晩中働いていたので、少し休憩が必要だと言いました。
  • She asked me if I knew the person who had called her earlier.
    • 彼女は、先ほど電話してきた人を私が知っているかどうか尋ねました。
  • They told us to arrive early because the meeting would start at nine sharp.
    • 彼らは、会議が9時ちょうどに始まるので早く来るようにと言いました。
  • My manager said he would review the document after he finished another task.
    • マネージャーは、別の作業を終えたらその書類を確認すると言いました。
  • She asked where we had bought the souvenirs that we showed her.
    • 彼女は、私たちが見せたお土産をどこで買ったのか尋ねました。

🧩 4. Practice Exercises（練習問題）

Exercise A – Choose the correct option

1. “I am tired.” → He said he ______ tired. (was / is / will be)
was
2. “Do you like sushi?” → She asked if I ______ sushi. (liked / like / had liked)
liked
3. “Close the window.” → He told me ______ the window. (closing / to close / close)
to close
4. “We will visit tomorrow.” → They said they ______ visit the next day. (would / will / had)
would
5. “Where did he go?” → She asked where he ______. (goes / had gone / went)
had gone

Exercise B – Complete the sentence

1. “I finished the work.” → He said he ______ ______ the work.
had finished
2. “Please call me later.” → She asked me ______ ______ her later.
to call
3. “Did you see my message?” → He asked if I ______ his message.
had seen
4. “We are moving next month.” → They said they ______ moving the next month.
were
5. “Don’t touch that!” → She told me ______ ______ that.
not to touch

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

Team Meeting About a New Project

Last week, our team had a meeting to discuss a new project. During the meeting, our manager said that the company would launch a new service in the summer, and he explained that we needed to prepare several documents before the end of the month. He also told us to check all customer data carefully, because some information might have changed.

After that, one of my colleagues asked if we could get extra support from another department. The manager said he would ask them, but he couldn’t promise anything yet. Later, another teammate asked where the training sessions would take place, and the manager said they would be held in the main office. At the end of the meeting, he asked us to send him our progress reports by Friday. When I returned to my desk, I realized I should have written down more details, so I listened to the recording again to make sure I didn’t miss anything important.

新プロジェクトに関するチーム会議

先週、私たちのチームは新しいプロジェクトについて話し合う会議を行いました。 会議中、マネージャーは会社が夏に新しいサービスを開始すると言い、月末までにいくつかの書類を準備する必要があると説明しました。 また、顧客データが変更されている可能性があるため、すべてを注意深く確認するようにと言いました。

その後、同僚の一人が、別の部署から追加のサポートを受けられるか尋ねました。 マネージャーは依頼してみると言いましたが、まだ確約はできないとも言いました。 別のメンバーは研修がどこで行われるのか尋ね、マネージャーは本社で行われると答えました。 会議の最後に、金曜日までに進捗報告を送るように頼まれました。 席に戻ったとき、もっと詳細を書いておくべきだったと気づき、重要な点を聞き逃していないか確認するために録音をもう一度聞きました。

❓ 6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

1) What did the manager say about the new service?
He said the company would launch it in the summer.
2) Why did the team need to check customer data?
Because some information might have changed.
3) What did a colleague ask about extra support?
He asked if they could get support from another department.
4) Where would the training sessions take place?
In the main office.
5) Why did the writer listen to the recording again?
Because they felt they should have written down more details.

🎯 7. Summary（まとめ）

  • Backshifting: present → past, will → would.
    • 時制が一つ過去に下がる。
  • Reported questions use normal word order.
    • 疑問文の語順は平叙文に戻す。
  • Commands use told/asked + object + to.
    • 命令・依頼は told/asked + 人 + to。
  • Pronouns and time expressions change.
    • 代名詞・時間表現が変わる。
