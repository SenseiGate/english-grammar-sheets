CEFR: B1-B2

Lesson 12: Linking Words

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

1. Contrast（対比）– although / however / despite

Although + sentence → 「〜だけれども」 → 文と文をつなぐ。

However + comma → 「しかし」 → 文頭・文中で使う。前の文と対比。

Despite + noun/gerund → 「〜にもかかわらず」 → 文ではなく名詞句をつなぐ。

Linking WordExample
althoughAlthough it was cold, we went hiking.
howeverIt was cold; however, we went hiking.
despiteDespite the cold weather, we went hiking.

2. Addition & Result（追加・結果）– in addition / therefore

In addition = extra information → 「さらに」

Therefore = result → 「その結果」

Linking WordExample
in additionShe speaks English. In addition, she speaks French.
thereforeHe was sick; therefore, he stayed home.

3. Condition（条件）– unless

Unless = except if → 「〜しない限り」「〜でなければ」

Linking WordExample
unlessWe can’t start unless everyone is here.

4. Common Japanese Learner Errors（日本人学習者のよくある間違い）

  • ❌ “Despite it was raining…” → 正しくは “Despite the rain…”
  • ❌ Using “however” like “but” inside a sentence → however は文頭か文中で区切る。
  • ❌ “Unless” = “if not” だが、二重否定に注意。

✏️ 2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • Although it was late, we kept talking.
    遅かったけれど話し続けました。
  • It was raining; however, we went out.
    雨でしたが外出しました。
  • She was tired; therefore, she went to bed early.
    疲れていたので早く寝ました。
  • In addition, he offered to help us.
    さらに、彼は手伝うと言ってくれました。
  • We can’t finish unless you join us.
    あなたが参加しない限り終わりません。
  • Despite the noise, I could concentrate.
    騒音にもかかわらず集中できました。
  • Although he studied hard, he didn’t pass.
    一生懸命勉強したのに合格しませんでした。

📝 3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • Although I had a busy schedule, I managed to finish the project before the deadline.
    忙しい予定だったにもかかわらず、締め切り前にプロジェクトを終えました。
  • The weather was terrible; however, the event continued as planned.
    天気は最悪でしたが、イベントは予定通り行われました。
  • She saved money for months; therefore, she was able to buy a new laptop.
    彼女は数か月間お金を貯めたので、新しいノートパソコンを買うことができました。
  • He is studying French. In addition, he takes weekly conversation lessons.
    彼はフランス語を勉強しています。さらに、週1回の会話レッスンも受けています。
  • Despite feeling nervous, she gave a confident presentation.
    緊張していたにもかかわらず、自信のあるプレゼンをしました。

🧩 4. Practice Exercises（練習問題）

Exercise A – Choose the correct option

1. ______ it was raining, we played soccer. (Although / Therefore / Unless)
Although
2. He was tired; ______, he kept working. (however / unless / despite)
however
3. She studied hard; ______, she passed the exam. (therefore / although / despite)
therefore
4. ______ you hurry, we will miss the train. (Unless / Despite / However)
Unless
5. ______ the cold weather, they went jogging. (Despite / Although / Therefore)
Despite

Exercise B – Complete the sentence

1. ______ it was difficult, he didn’t give up.
Although
2. She was sick; ______, she stayed home.
therefore
3. ______ the noise, I finished my homework.
Despite
4. In addition, he ______ to join the team.
offered
5. We can’t start ______ everyone arrives.
unless

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

Preparing for a Challenging Team Presentation

Last month, our team began preparing for a large presentation that would be shown to several departments. Although we had a clear plan, unexpected problems appeared almost every day. The computer system crashed twice; however, we continued working and tried to stay positive. In addition, two new members joined the team, which made communication a bit more complicated. Despite these challenges, everyone tried their best to stay organized and focused.

On the day of the presentation, we were nervous; therefore, we arrived early to check the equipment. The room was cold and crowded, but we managed to set everything up on time. We felt confident that the presentation would go smoothly, as long as everyone stayed focused. In the end, everything worked perfectly, and the managers praised our teamwork. Despite the stress, the experience helped us grow and improved our ability to handle difficult situations.

困難なチームプレゼンの準備

先月、私たちのチームは複数の部署に向けて行う大きなプレゼンの準備を始めました。 計画は明確でしたが、予想外の問題がほぼ毎日のように起こりました。 コンピューターシステムが2回も落ちましたが、それでも前向きに作業を続けました。 さらに、新しいメンバーが2人加わり、コミュニケーションが少し複雑になりました。 それでも皆、整理しながら集中しようと努力しました。

プレゼン当日、私たちは緊張していたので、機材チェックのために早めに会場に到着しました。 部屋は寒くて混んでいましたが、なんとか時間通りに準備を終えました。 全員が集中し続ける限り、プレゼンはうまくいくと感じていました。 結果的にすべて完璧に進み、マネージャーたちからチームワークを褒められました。 ストレスはありましたが、この経験は私たちを成長させ、難しい状況への対応力を高めてくれました。

❓ 6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

1) What unexpected problem happened during preparation?
The computer system crashed twice.
2) Why did communication become more complicated?
Because two new members joined the team.
3) Why did the team arrive early on presentation day?
To check the equipment.
4) What made the team feel confident on presentation day?
They felt confident as long as everyone stayed focused.
5) What did the managers praise?
Their teamwork.

🎯 7. Summary（まとめ）

  • Although / however / despite show contrast.
    • 対比を表す。
  • Therefore shows result.
    • 結果を表す。
  • In addition adds information.
    • 情報を追加する。
  • Unless means “except if.”
    • 「〜しない限り」を意味する。
