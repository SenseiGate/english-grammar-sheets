CEFR: B1-B2

Lesson 1: Present Perfect vs Past Simple

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

1. Present Perfect（現在完了）

We use the Present Perfect (have/has + past participle) to talk about experiences, results, or actions connected to now. → 今につながる経験・結果・継続を表す。

UseExample
ExperienceI have visited Spain three times.
ResultShe has lost her keys.
Unfinished timeWe have studied a lot this week.

Common words: ever / never / already / yet / just / recently / so far

2. Past Simple（過去形）

We use the Past Simple (V2 / past form) for actions that are finished and in a finished time. → 終わった時間の中で完了した出来事。

UseExample
Finished actionI visited Spain last year.
Past storyShe lost her keys yesterday.
Specific timeWe studied a lot last week.

Common words: yesterday / last year / in 2010 / when I was a child

3. Typical Japanese Learner Errors（よくある間違い）

  • ❌ I have been to Tokyo yesterday. → ✔️ I went to Tokyo yesterday.
  • ❌ I went to Tokyo many times. → ✔️ I have been to Tokyo many times.

✏️ 2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • I have finished my homework.
    • 宿題を終えたところです。
  • I finished my homework an hour ago.
    • 1時間前に終えました。
  • She has never visited London.
    • ロンドンに行ったことがありません。
  • She visited London in 2019.
    • 2019年に訪れました。
  • We have just eaten dinner.
    • ちょうど夕食を食べたところです。
  • We ate dinner at seven.
    • 7時に夕食を食べました。
  • They have lived in Osaka for ten years.
    • 10年間大阪に住んでいます。

📝 3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • I have studied English for many years, but I started online lessons last year.
    • 何年も英語を勉強していますが、オンラインは去年始めました。
  • He has already finished the report, but he sent it only a few minutes ago.
    • 彼はレポートを終えていますが、送ったのは数分前です。
  • We have visited that museum many times, but we went again during the holidays.
    • 何度も行ったことがありますが、休暇中にまた行きました。
  • She has never driven abroad, so she felt nervous when she drove in Canada.
    • 海外で運転したことがなかったので、カナダで緊張しました。
  • They have just moved to a new apartment, but they bought their old house ten years ago.
    • 新しいアパートに引っ越したばかりですが、前の家を買ったのは10年前です。

🧩 4. Practice Exercises（練習問題）

Exercise A – Choose the correct option

1. I (have visited / visited) Kyoto three times.
have visited
2. I (have visited / visited) Kyoto last summer.
visited
3. She (has just finished / just finished) her homework.
has just finished
4. We (have watched / watched) that movie yesterday.
watched
5. They (have lived / lived) in Tokyo from 2010 to 2015.
lived

Exercise B – Complete the sentence

1. I have already ________ (eat) breakfast.
have already eaten
2. We ________ (go) to Okinawa in 2022.
went
3. She ________ never ________ (see) snow before this winter.
has never seen
4. They ________ (move) to Osaka last month.
moved
5. I ________ (lose) my keys yesterday, but I ________ (find) them now.
lost / have found

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

How My Travel Style Has Changed

I have always loved traveling, but my travel style has changed over the years. When I was a university student, I traveled on a very low budget. I stayed in cheap hostels, ate simple meals, and walked everywhere. I visited many cities, but I didn’t have much time to understand each place.

In the last five years, my situation has changed. I have started working full-time, and I have saved some money. I have visited several countries again, but this time I stayed longer in each city. For example, I went to Barcelona last year and stayed for three weeks. I have made local friends, and I still keep in touch with them online.

変化してきた私の旅行スタイル

私は昔から旅行が大好きですが、年々そのスタイルは変わってきました。大学生のときは節約旅行で、 安いホステルに泊まり、簡単な食事をとり、どこへでも歩いて行きました。多くの街を訪れましたが、 深く理解する時間はあまりありませんでした。

この5年間で状況は変わりました。フルタイムで働き始め、お金も貯まりました。同じ国を再び訪れましたが、 今度は一つの街に長く滞在しました。例えば、去年はバルセロナに3週間滞在しました。現地の友達もでき、 今でもオンラインで連絡を取り合っています。

❓ 6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

1. How did the writer travel as a student?
On a low budget, staying in cheap hostels.
2. Why didn’t the writer understand each place well?
Because they didn’t have much time in each city.
3. What has changed in the last five years?
They started working full-time and saved money.
4. How is the writer’s travel style different now?
They stay longer in each city and make local friends.
5. Why is traveling more meaningful now?
Because they made local friends and stay longer.

🎯 7. Summary（まとめ）

  • Present Perfect = experience, result, unfinished time.
    • 今につながる動作。
  • Past Simple = finished actions in finished time.
    • 終わった時間の中の動作。
  • Don’t use Present Perfect with yesterday / last year.
    • 「昨日」「去年」などとは使わない。
  • Use Present Perfect for life experience.
    • 経験に使う。
  • Check time expressions first.
    • 時間表現がヒント。
