CEFR: A1

Lesson 40: Object Pronouns

📚1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

1) What are object pronouns?

Object pronouns are words that replace the object of a verb. We use them after the verb.

目的語を表す代名詞です。動詞の後に置きます。

2) List of object pronouns

SubjectObjectJapanese
Ime私を／私に
youyouあなたを／あなたに
hehim彼を／彼に
sheher彼女を／彼女に
ititそれを／それに
weus私たちを／私たちに
theythem彼らを／彼らに

3) Position in the sentence

  • I help them.
  • She calls me.
  • We like him.

目的格代名詞は動詞の後に置きます。「help them」「call me」など。

✏️2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • I see him every morning.
    私は毎朝彼を見ます。
  • She helps us with homework.
    彼女は宿題で私たちを助けてくれます。
  • They like her a lot.
    彼らは彼女のことがとても好きです。
  • The teacher calls me to the front.
    先生は私を前に呼びます。
  • Can you hear them now?
    今、彼らの声が聞こえますか。
  • We visit them after school.
    私たちは放課後に彼らのところへ行きます。
  • Please give it to me.
    それを私にください。

📝3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • The teacher asks us a question, and we answer her slowly.
    先生は私たちに質問をして、私たちはゆっくり彼女に答えます。
  • I see my friends in the hallway, and I wave to them.
    廊下で友達を見かけて、私は彼らに手を振ります。
  • My classmate is new, so I help him find his classroom.
    クラスメイトは新しい生徒なので、私は彼が教室を見つけるのを手伝います。
  • Our teacher gives us a small test, but she tells us not to worry.
    先生は私たちに小テストを出しますが、心配しないでと言ってくれます。
  • After school, my friends wait for me, and I walk home with them.
    放課後、友達は私を待ってくれて、私は彼らと一緒に家に帰ります。

🧩4. Practice Exercises（練習問題）

Exercise A: Choose the correct option
（正しい形を選んでください）

  1. Tom is new. Please help (he / him).
    2. him
  2. The teacher sees (we / us) in the library.
    3. us
  3. I cannot hear (they / them) from here.
    4. them
  4. Can you give (I / me) that book?
    5. me
  5. She likes (you / your) very much.
    6. you

Exercise B: Complete the sentences
（文を完成させてください）

  1. The teacher helps ______ after class.
    2. us
  2. I see my friends. I want to talk to ______.
    3. them
  3. This book is for you. I will give ______ to you later.
    4. it
  4. My parents call ______ every evening.
    5. me
  5. We are new students. Please sit with ______ at lunch.
    6. us

📖5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

Helping Them at School

Today at school, we have a new student in our class. His name is Ken, and he looks a little shy. The teacher asks us to help him, so I sit next to him. I show him the classroom and the library. At lunch, my friends and I invite him to eat with us. We talk to him and listen to him. He smiles and thanks us. Now he is happy, and we are happy too.

学校で助けてあげる

今日、学校でクラスに新しい生徒が来ました。彼の名前はケンで、少し恥ずかしそうに見えます。 先生は私たちに彼を助けるように言うので、私は彼の隣に座ります。 私は彼に教室と図書室を見せます。昼休みには、友達と一緒に彼を昼食に誘います。 私たちは彼に話しかけ、彼の話を聞きます。彼は笑って私たちにお礼を言います。 今、彼も私たちも嬉しい気持ちです。

❓6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

  1. Who is the new student?

    2. 新しい生徒は誰ですか？

    His name is Ken.
  2. How does Ken look at the beginning?

    3. ケンは最初どのように見えますか？

    He looks a little shy.
  3. What does the teacher ask the class to do?

    4. 先生はクラスに何をするように頼みますか？

    The teacher asks them to help him.
  4. Where do they invite Ken at lunch?

    5. 昼食のとき、彼らはケンをどこに誘いますか？

    They invite him to eat with them.
  5. How does Ken feel at the end of the story?

    6. 物語の終わりにケンはどのように感じていますか？

    He feels happy.

🎯Summary（まとめ）

  • me / him / her / us / them are object pronouns.
    目的格代名詞は me・him・her・us・them などです。
  • They usually come after the verb.
    目的格代名詞はふつう動詞の後に置きます。
  • They help avoid repeating nouns.
    名詞のくり返しを防ぐために使います。
  • We often use them when talking about friends and teachers.
    友達や先生について話すときによく使います。
