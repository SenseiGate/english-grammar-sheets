CEFR: A1

Lesson 31: Future with going to

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

We use “going to” to talk about future plans and things we see will happen.

「going to」は、未来の計画や、起こりそうなことを話すときに使います。

am / is / are + going to + base verb is the basic form.

基本形は am / is / are + going to + 動詞の原形 です。

FormExampleMeaning
PositiveI am going to study.私は勉強するつもりです。
NegativeShe is not going to come.彼女は来るつもりはありません。
QuestionAre you going to go?あなたは行くつもりですか。

Plans: things we decided before now.

計画：前から決めていたこと。

Predictions with evidence: we see or know something now.

根拠のある予想：今見えていること・分かっていることからの予想。

✏️ 2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • I am going to study tonight.

    私は今夜勉強するつもりです。

  • She is going to visit her grandmother.

    彼女はおばあさんを訪ねるつもりです。

  • They are going to play soccer after school.

    彼らは放課後サッカーをするつもりです。

  • We are going to have a party on Saturday.

    私たちは土曜日にパーティーをするつもりです。

  • He is not going to watch TV today.

    彼は今日はテレビを見るつもりはありません。

  • It is going to rain soon.

    もうすぐ雨が降りそうです。

  • Are you going to do your homework now?

    今宿題をするつもりですか。

📝 3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • I am going to get up early on Saturday and go to the park.

    私は土曜日に早く起きて、公園に行くつもりです。

  • My sister is going to make pancakes for breakfast, and we are going to eat together.

    妹は朝ごはんにパンケーキを作って、私たちは一緒に食べるつもりです。

  • They are going to study English in the morning and play games in the afternoon.

    彼らは午前中に英語を勉強して、午後にゲームをするつもりです。

  • It is very cloudy, so it is going to rain this evening.

    とても曇っているので、今晩雨が降りそうです。

  • We are not going to go to the park if it is going to rain.

    雨が降りそうなら、公園には行かないつもりです。

🧩 4. Practice Exercises（練習問題）

Exercise A – Choose the correct option

正しい文を選びましょう。

1) I ______ going to read a book tonight.

a) am　b) is

a) am

2) She ______ going to visit her friend.

a) are　b) is

b) is

3) They ______ going to play soccer after school.

a) is　b) are

b) are

4) It ______ going to rain soon.

a) is　b) are

a) is

5) ______ you going to do your homework now?

a) Are　b) Is

a) Are

Exercise B – Complete the sentence

正しい形を入れて文を完成させましょう。（am going to / is going to / are going to）

1) I ______ (study) English tonight.

am going to study

2) She ______ (visit) her grandmother on Sunday.

is going to visit

3) They ______ (play) soccer after school.

are going to play

4) It is very cloudy. It ______ (rain) soon.

is going to rain

5) We ______ (not watch) TV tonight.

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

My Plans for Saturday

On Saturday, I am going to get up at seven. I am going to eat breakfast with my family. After that, I am going to go to the park with my friend Ken. We are going to play soccer and take many pictures. In the afternoon, I am going to go home and do my homework. In the evening, my family is going to watch a movie together. We are going to eat popcorn and drink juice. It is going to be a fun day.

土曜日の予定

土曜日、私は7時に起きるつもりです。家族と朝ごはんを食べます。 そのあと、友だちのケンと公園に行くつもりです。サッカーをして、たくさん写真を撮ります。 午後は家に帰って、宿題をするつもりです。 夕方、家族と一緒に映画を見るつもりです。ポップコーンを食べて、ジュースを飲みます。 とても楽しい一日になりそうです。

❓ 6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

1) What time is the writer going to get up on Saturday?

書き手は土曜日に何時に起きる予定ですか？

The writer is going to get up at seven.

2) Who is the writer going to go to the park with?

書き手は誰と公園へ行く予定ですか？

The writer is going to go with his friend Ken.

3) What are they going to do in the park?

彼らは公園で何をする予定ですか？

They are going to play soccer and take pictures.

4) What is the writer going to do in the afternoon?

書き手は午後に何をする予定ですか？

He is going to go home and do his homework.

5) What is the family going to do in the evening?

家族は夕方に何をする予定ですか？

They are going to watch a movie, eat popcorn, and drink juice.

🎯 Summary（まとめ）

  • am / is / are + going to + base verb is used for future plans.
    未来の計画には am / is / are + going to + 動詞の原形 を使います。
  • am not / isn't / aren't going to is used for negative plans.
    しないつもりのときは am not / isn't / aren't going to を使います。
  • Are / Is + subject + going to? is used for questions.
    疑問文は Are / Is + 主語 + going to の順になります。
  • going to is used for plans decided before now or predictions with evidence.
    前から決めていたことや、根拠のある予想に going to を使います。
