CEFR: A1

Lesson 30: Past of be

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

We use “was” and “were” to talk about the past.

「was」「were」は、過去のことを言うときに使います。

was is used with I, he, she, it.

I / he / she / it と一緒に was を使います。

were is used with you, we, they.

you / we / they と一緒に were を使います。

SubjectPast formExample
I / he / she / itwasShe was happy.
you / we / theywereThey were tired.

Negative: was not (wasn’t), were not (weren’t).

否定：was not / were not（wasn’t / weren’t）。

Questions: Was / Were + subject...?

疑問文：Was / Were + 主語 の順になります。

✏️ 2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • I was at home yesterday.

    私は昨日家にいました。

  • She was very happy.

    彼女はとても幸せでした。

  • He was not busy.

    彼は忙しくありませんでした。

  • They were at school.

    彼らは学校にいました。

  • We were tired after the game.

    私たちは試合のあと疲れていました。

  • You were not late.

    あなたは遅れていませんでした。

  • Were you in Tokyo last week?

    あなたは先週東京にいましたか。

📝 3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • I was very tired last night, so I went to bed early.

    私は昨夜とても疲れていたので、早く寝ました。

  • My friends were at the park, but I was at home.

    友だちは公園にいましたが、私は家にいました。

  • She was not happy because the weather was bad.

    天気が悪かったので、彼女はうれしくありませんでした。

  • We were in the library, and it was very quiet there.

    私たちは図書館にいて、そこはとても静かでした。

  • Were they in your class last year, or were they in another class?

    彼らは去年あなたのクラスでしたか、それとも別のクラスでしたか。

🧩 4. Practice Exercises（練習問題）

Exercise A – Choose the correct option

正しい形（was / were / wasn’t / weren’t）を選びましょう。

1) I ______ at the park yesterday.

a) was　b) were

a) was

2) They ______ very happy.

a) was　b) were

b) were

3) She ______ not at school.

a) was　b) were

a) was

4) ______ you tired last night?

a) Was　b) Were

b) Were

5) They ______ not in the classroom.

a) wasn’t　b) weren’t

b) weren’t

正しい形（was / were / wasn't / weren't）を入れましょう。

1) I ______ very tired last night.

was

were

3) She ______ happy because the weather was bad.

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

I Was at the Museum

Yesterday was a special day. I was at the museum with my family. The museum was big and very quiet. There were many old pictures and interesting statues. My sister was excited, but my brother was a little bored. My parents were happy because the tickets were cheap. At noon, we were hungry, so we went to the museum café. The food was simple but good. In the evening, we were tired, but we were glad. It was a nice day.

博物館にいました

昨日は特別な日でした。私は家族と一緒に博物館にいました。 博物館は大きくて、とても静かでした。古い絵やおもしろい像がたくさんありました。 妹はわくわくしていましたが、弟は少し退屈そうでした。 両親はチケットが安かったので、うれしそうでした。 昼ごろおなかがすいたので、博物館のカフェに行きました。 食べ物はシンプルでしたが、おいしかったです。 夕方には私たちは疲れていましたが、来てよかったと思いました。 とてもよい一日でした。

❓ 6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

1) Where was the writer yesterday?

書き手は昨日どこにいましたか？

The writer was at the museum.

2) How was the museum?

その博物館はどうでしたか？

It was big and very quiet.

3) Who was excited at the museum?

博物館で誰がわくわくしていましたか？

The writer’s sister was excited.

4) Where did they go when they were hungry?

お腹がすいたとき、彼らはどこへ行きましたか？

They went to the museum café.

5) How did they feel in the evening?

夕方、彼らはどのように感じていましたか？

They were tired but glad.

🎯 Summary（まとめ）

  • was is used with I, he, she, it.
    I / he / she / it と一緒に was を使います。
  • were is used with you, we, they.
    you / we / they と一緒に were を使います。
  • was not / were not are the negative forms.
    否定形は was not / were not（wasn't / weren't）です。
  • Was / Were + subject is used for questions.
    疑問文は Was / Were + 主語 の順になります。
