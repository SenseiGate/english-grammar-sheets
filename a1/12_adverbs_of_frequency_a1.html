CEFR: A1

Lesson 12: Adverbs of Frequency

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

Adverbs of frequency show how often something happens.

頻度副詞は、行動がどれくらい起こるかを表します。

AdverbMeaningFrequency
alwaysいつも100%
usuallyたいてい90%
oftenよく70%
sometimesときどき50%
rarelyめったに〜ない10%
never決して〜ない0%

With normal verbs, the adverb goes before the verb.

一般動詞の前に置きます。

I always eat breakfast.

With the verb “be,” the adverb goes after am / is / are.

be 動詞の後に置きます。

She is usually happy.

✏️ 2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • I always eat breakfast.
    私はいつも朝ごはんを食べます。
  • He often plays soccer.
    彼はよくサッカーをします。
  • We usually walk to school.
    私たちはたいてい歩いて学校へ行きます。
  • She sometimes reads comics.
    彼女はときどき漫画を読みます。
  • They rarely watch TV.
    彼らはめったにテレビを見ません。
  • I never drink coffee.
    私はコーヒーを決して飲みません。
  • My father is usually busy.
    私の父はたいてい忙しいです。

📝 3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • I always study English after dinner at my desk.
    私はいつも夕食の後に机で英語を勉強します。
  • My sister often listens to music when she does her homework.
    私の姉（妹）は宿題をするときによく音楽を聞きます。
  • We usually play basketball in the park on Saturday afternoon.
    私たちはたいてい土曜日の午後に公園でバスケットボールをします。
  • Our teacher is sometimes late because the train is crowded.
    電車が混んでいるので、先生はときどき遅れます。
  • My grandparents never use a smartphone, but they often call us.
    祖父母はスマートフォンを使いませんが、よく私たちに電話をします。

🧩 4. Practice Exercises（練習問題）

Exercise A – Choose the correct option

正しい頻度副詞を選びましょう。

1. I ___ eat rice for breakfast. (always / never)

always

2. She is ___ tired. (usually / rarely)

usually

3. They ___ play games on Sunday. (often / never)

often

4. My brother ___ drinks milk. (sometimes / always)

sometimes

5. Our teacher is ___ late. (never / often)

never

Exercise B – Complete the sentence

頻度副詞を使って文を完成させましょう。

1. I __________ drink tea in the evening.

I sometimes drink tea in the evening.

2. She __________ eats breakfast before school.

She always eats breakfast before school.

3. They __________ go to the library after class.

They often go to the library after class.

4. My father __________ watches TV at night.

My father rarely watches TV at night.

5. We __________ are late for school.

We are never late for school.

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

How Often I Study

I always study English after dinner. I sit at my desk and open my notebook. I usually read my textbook and write new words. I often listen to short English songs. On Saturday, I sometimes study with my friend online. I never study very late at night.

どれくらい勉強するか

私はいつも夕食の後に英語を勉強します。机に座ってノートを開きます。たいてい教科書を読んで新しい単語を書きます。よく短い英語の歌を聞きます。土曜日にはときどき友だちとオンラインで勉強します。夜遅くまで勉強することは決してありません。

❓ 6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

1. When does the writer study English?

書き手はいつ英語を勉強しますか。

After dinner.

2. Where does the writer sit?

書き手はどこに座りますか。

At a desk.

3. What does the writer usually read?

書き手はたいてい何を読みますか。

A textbook.

4. Who does the writer study with on Saturday?

土曜日に誰と勉強しますか。

A friend.

5. Does the writer study late at night?

夜遅くまで勉強しますか。

No, never.

🎯 Summary（まとめ）

  • Adverbs of frequency show how often something happens.
    頻度副詞は行動の頻度を表します。
  • always / usually / often / sometimes / rarely / never are common.
    よく使う頻度副詞です。
  • Place the adverb before normal verbs.
    一般動詞の前に置きます。
  • Place the adverb after am / is / are.
    be 動詞の後に置きます。
