EIKEN: 4

E4: Daily Activities & Time（毎日の活動と時間）

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

1. Simple daily actions

We use basic verbs to talk about daily activities such as wake up, eat, go, study.

wake up, eat, go, study などの動詞で毎日の行動を表します。

  • I wake up at 7.
  • I eat breakfast.
  • You go to school.
  • I study English.

2. Telling time

We say the hour + minutes. Example: 7:30 → seven thirty

時間は「7:30 → seven thirty」のように読みます。

TimeEnglish
6:00six o'clock
6:15six fifteen
6:30six thirty
6:45six forty-five

3. Short schedules

We can describe our day using times and actions.

時間と言動作を組み合わせて1日のスケジュールを言えます。

  • I wake up at 7:00.
  • I go to school at 8:00.
  • I eat lunch at 12:30.

🔤 New vocabulary (useful for schedules)

schedule — スケジュール
usually — たいてい

Add these words to your sentences: "I usually eat breakfast at 7." / "My schedule is busy on weekdays."

✏️ 2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • I wake up at seven.
    • 私は7時に起きます。
  • You go to school at eight.
    • あなたは8時に学校へ行きます。
  • I eat breakfast at home.
    • 私は家で朝ごはんを食べます。
  • I study English after school.
    • 私は放課後に英語を勉強します。
  • You come home at four.
    • あなたは4時に帰ります。
  • I go to bed at ten.
    • 私は10時に寝ます。
  • It is seven thirty now.
    • 今は7時30分です。

📝 3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • I wake up at six thirty, and I eat breakfast with my family.
    • 私は6時30分に起きて、家族と朝ごはんを食べます。
  • You go to school at eight, but I go at eight thirty.
    • あなたは8時に学校へ行きますが、私は8時30分に行きます。
  • I study math at school, and I study English at home.
    • 私は学校で数学を勉強し、家で英語を勉強します。
  • I eat lunch at twelve fifteen with my friends.
    • 私は12時15分に友だちと昼ごはんを食べます。
  • I go to bed at ten because I am sleepy.
    • 私は眠いので10時に寝ます。
  • On Saturdays I wake up later, but on weekdays I follow my schedule because I have school and club activities.
    • 土曜日は遅く起きますが、平日は学校と部活があるのでスケジュールに従います。

🧩 4. Practice Exercises（練習問題）

Exercise A – Choose the correct option（正しい答えを選びましょう）

かっこ（　）の中から正しいほうをえらびましょう。

  1. I never stay in bed late on school days. I wake up at ( six / ten ).
    six
  2. Classes start early in the morning. You go to school at ( eight / three ).
    eight
  3. The lunch bell rings in the middle of the day. I eat lunch at ( twelve / five ).
    twelve
  4. I check my watch in the morning before school. It is ( seven thirty / ten o'clock ).
    seven thirty
  5. I am tired after a long day of school and homework. I go to bed at ( ten / one ).
    ten

Exercise B – Complete the sentence（文を完成させましょう）

wake up / eat / go / study / at を使って文を完成させましょう。

  1. I ______ ______ seven.
    I wake up at seven.
  2. I ______ lunch ______ school.
    I eat lunch at school.
  3. You ______ to school ______ eight.
    You go to school at eight.
  4. I ______ English ______ home.
    I study English at home.
  5. I ______ to bed ______ ten.
    I go to bed at ten.

Exercise C – Production (write 2–3 sentences)

次の問いに対して英語で2〜3文書いてみましょう。例は下の「Show Sample」。

  1. Describe your morning routine. (2–3 sentences)
  2. What time do you eat lunch and why? (1–2 sentences)
  3. When do you go to bed? Give one reason. (1 sentence)
Sample:
I wake up at 6:30 and eat breakfast with my family. I go to school at 8:00 and study until 4:00. I eat lunch at 12:15 because I have club practice in the afternoon.

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

My Daily Schedule

My name is Yuto, and this is my daily schedule. I wake up at six thirty every morning. I eat breakfast at seven, and I go to school at eight. At school, I study math, science, and English. I eat lunch with my friends at twelve fifteen. After school, I go home at four and do my homework. I eat dinner at seven and go to bed at ten. This is my day.

私の1日のスケジュール

私の名前はゆうとで、これが私の1日のスケジュールです。毎朝6時30分に起きます。 7時に朝ごはんを食べ、8時に学校へ行きます。 学校では数学、理科、英語を勉強します。 12時15分に友だちと昼ごはんを食べます。 放課後は4時に帰って宿題をします。 7時に夕ごはんを食べ、10時に寝ます。 これが私の1日です。

❓ 6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

  1. What time does Yuto wake up?
    ゆうとは何時に起きますか？
    He wakes up at six thirty.
  2. What time does he go to school?
    ゆうとは何時に学校へ行きますか？
    He goes to school at eight.
  3. What subjects does he study?
    どんな教科を勉強しますか？
    Math, science, and English.
  4. What time does he eat lunch?
    ゆうとは何時に昼ごはんを食べますか？
    At twelve fifteen.
  5. What does Yuto do after school?
    ゆうとは放課後に何をしますか？
    He goes home and does his homework.
  6. What time does he go to bed?
    ゆうとは何時に寝ますか？
    He goes to bed at ten.

🎯 Summary（まとめ）

  • Use simple verbs to talk about daily actions.
    • 毎日の行動には基本動詞を使います。
  • Tell time using "hour + minutes".
    • 時間は「時＋分」で読みます。
  • You can describe your day with times and actions.
    • 時間と動作で1日の流れを説明できます。
  • Practice saying your schedule in English and write short paragraphs to show control of connectors like because/and/but.
    • because / and / but を使った短い段落を書く練習をしましょう。
