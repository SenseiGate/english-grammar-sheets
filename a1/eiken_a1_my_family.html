E6: My Family

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

1. Family members and "be" verbs（家族の言い方と be 動詞）

We use be verbs (am / is / are) to talk about our family. → 家族について話すときに be 動詞（am / is / are）を使います。

EnglishJapaneseExample
mother母This is my mother.
father父He is my father.
sister / brother妹・姉 / 兄・弟She is my sister.

2. Possessive adjectives（所有形容詞：my / your / his / her / our / their）

We use my, your, his, her, our, their to show who something belongs to. → だれのものかを表すときに my / your / his / her / our / their を使います。

PersonPossessiveExample
ImyMy family is small.
youyourYour brother is kind.
hehisHis father is a teacher.
sheherHer sister is funny.
weourOur grandparents live in Osaka.
theytheirTheir mother is busy.

3. Describing family with adjectives（家族を形容詞で説明する）

We use adjectives like kind, funny, tall, busy with be verbs. → kind, funny, tall, busy などの形容詞を be 動詞といっしょに使います。

My mother is kind. / My brother is funny. → 母はやさしいです。／兄はおもしろいです。

✏️ 2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • This is my family.
    • これは私の家族です。
  • My father is a teacher.
    • 私の父は先生です。
  • My mother is kind.
    • 私の母はやさしいです。
  • My sister is funny.
    • 私の妹（姉）はおもしろいです。
  • We are a happy family.
    • 私たちは仲のよい家族です。
  • Your brother is very tall.
    • あなたの兄（弟）はとても背が高いです。
  • Their grandparents are old but very active.
    • 彼らの祖父母は年をとっていますが、とても元気です。

📝 3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • My family is small because I am an only child.
    • 私は一人っ子なので、私の家族は小さいです。
  • My father is a doctor, and he is very busy on weekdays.
    • 父は医者で、平日はとても忙しいです。
  • My mother is kind and patient, and she helps me with my homework.
    • 母はやさしくてがまん強く、宿題を手伝ってくれます。
  • My grandparents live in another city, but we visit them on weekends.
    • 祖父母は別の町に住んでいますが、週末に会いに行きます。
  • We often eat dinner together and talk about our day.
    • 私たちはよく一緒に夕食を食べて、一日のことを話します。

🧩 4. Practice Exercises（練習問題）

Exercise A – Choose the correct option（正しい答えを選びましょう）

1. This is ______ mother. (my / I)
my
2. ______ father is a doctor. (Her / She)
Her
3. My grandparents ______ kind. (is / are)
are
4. This is ______ brother. (his / he)
his
5. Our family ______ small. (is / are)
is

Exercise B – Complete the sentence（文を完成させましょう）

1. This is ______ sister. (my)
my
2. My father ______ a teacher. (be)
is
3. Her grandparents ______ in Tokyo. (live)
live
4. Our family ______ dinner together every night. (eat)
eats
5. Their mother is ______ and funny. (kind)
kind

Exercise C – Production (write 1–2 sentences)

Write short answers in English (1–2 sentences):

  1. How many people are in your family?
  2. Describe one family member using my / his / her and one adjective.
Sample:
There are four people in my family. My mother is kind and my father is funny.

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

My Family

My name is Hana, and this is my family. There are four people in my family: my father, my mother, my younger brother, and me. My father is a company worker, and he is very busy on weekdays. My mother is a nurse, and she is kind and patient. My brother is ten years old, and he is funny and likes games. We are a happy family, and we often eat dinner together and talk about our day.

younger brother — a brother who is younger than you
patient — able to wait calmly

私の家族

私の名前ははなです。これが私の家族です。私の家族は4人で、父、母、弟、そして私です。父は会社員で、平日はとても忙しいです。母は看護師で、やさしくてがまん強いです。弟は10歳で、おもしろくてゲームが好きです。私たちは仲のよい家族で、よく一緒に夕食を食べて一日のことを話します。

🎯 7. Summary（まとめ）

  • Use family words like mother, father, sister, brother, grandparents.
    • 母・父・兄弟・祖父母などの家族の語彙を使いましょう。
  • Use my / your / his / her / our / their to show who something belongs to.
    • 所有形容詞で「だれのものか」を表します。
  • Use be verbs + adjectives to describe family members.
    • be 動詞＋形容詞で家族の性格やようすを説明します。
  • Practice writing 1–2 sentences about your family to talk about them in English.
    • 家族について1〜2文書いて、英語で紹介する練習をしましょう。
