CEFR: A1

Lesson 38: Like / Love / Hate + ing

📚1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

1) What does “like / love / hate + ing” mean?

We use like, love, and hate with a verb + ing to talk about things we enjoy or do not enjoy in general.

like・love・hate ＋ 動詞 ing で、ふだん好きなこと・嫌いなことを表します。

2) Basic form

VerbStructureExample
likelike + ingI like reading.
lovelove + ingShe loves cooking.
hatehate + ingThey hate running.

like / love / hate の後ろは動詞の ing 形を使います。

3) Meaning in daily life

  • I like playing games.（ゲームをするのが好きです。）
  • I love listening to music.（音楽を聞くのが大好きです。）
  • I hate doing homework.（宿題をするのが嫌いです。）

自分の好みやきらいなことをシンプルに言うときに使います。

✏️2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • I like reading comics after school.
    私は放課後にマンガを読むのが好きです。
  • She loves playing soccer with her friends.
    彼女は友達とサッカーをするのが大好きです。
  • They hate cleaning their room.
    彼らは自分の部屋を掃除するのが嫌いです。
  • My brother likes watching movies at night.
    弟は夜に映画を見るのが好きです。
  • We love eating ice cream in summer.
    私たちは夏にアイスクリームを食べるのが大好きです。
  • He hates getting up early on Sunday.
    彼は日曜日に早起きするのが嫌いです。
  • Our teacher likes listening to music quietly.
    先生は静かに音楽を聞くのが好きです。

📝3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • On weekends, I like staying at home and reading books slowly in my room.
    週末、私は家にいて、自分の部屋でゆっくり本を読むのが好きです。
  • My sister loves playing the piano, and she practices carefully every evening.
    姉はピアノを弾くのが大好きで、毎晩注意深く練習します。
  • My friend hates doing homework, but he knows it is important for school.
    友達は宿題をするのが嫌いですが、学校のために大切だと分かっています。
  • We love going to the park and walking slowly while we talk about our day.
    私たちは公園に行き、ゆっくり歩きながら一日のことを話すのが大好きです。
  • Our parents like cooking dinner together, and they work quietly and carefully in the kitchen.
    両親は一緒に夕食を作るのが好きで、キッチンで静かに注意深く作業します。

🧩4. Practice Exercises（練習問題）

Exercise A: Choose the correct option
（正しいほうを選んでください）

  1. I always smile when I play video games. I ______ playing them after school. (like / hate)
    2. like
  2. She ______ getting up early on Sunday. She always looks tired and unhappy. (loves / hates)
    3. hates
  3. They always laugh and have fun at the cinema. They ______ watching movies together. (love / hate)
    4. love
  4. My brother ______ doing his homework. He always tries to avoid it. (likes / hates)
    5. hates
  5. We always feel warm and happy at the dinner table. We ______ eating dinner with our family. (like / hate)
    6. like

Exercise B: Complete the sentences
（文を完成させてください。like / love / hate + ing を使いましょう。）

  1. I ____________________ in the park after school.
    2. I like walking in the park after school.
  2. My sister ____________________ to music in her room.
    3. My sister loves listening to music in her room.
  3. They ____________________ their room on weekends.
    4. They hate cleaning their room on weekends.
  4. We ____________________ board games with our friends.
    5. We love playing board games with our friends.
  5. He ____________________ early on cold mornings.
    6. He hates getting up early on cold mornings.

📖5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

My Favorite Free Time

After school, I like having some quiet free time. I usually go to my room and listen to music. I love listening to soft songs while I sit on my bed. Sometimes I read comics slowly and laugh at the funny stories. On weekends, I like walking to the park with my friends. We love playing soccer and talking about games. I hate staying at home all day and doing nothing. I feel happy when I am moving and doing something I enjoy. My free time is simple, but I really love it.

好きな自由時間の過ごし方

放課後、私は静かな自由時間を持つのが好きです。たいてい自分の部屋に行き、音楽を聞きます。 ベッドに座りながら、やさしい曲を聞くのが大好きです。ときどきマンガをゆっくり読んで、おもしろい話に笑います。 週末には、友達と公園まで歩くのが好きです。私たちはサッカーをしたり、ゲームの話をしたりするのが大好きです。 一日中家にいて何もしないのは嫌いです。体を動かして好きなことをしているとき、私は幸せだと感じます。 私の自由時間はシンプルですが、本当に気に入っています。

❓6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

  1. What does the writer like to do after school?

    2. 書き手は放課後に何をするのが好きですか？

    The writer likes having quiet free time, listening to music, and reading comics.
  2. Where does the writer usually listen to music?

    3. 書き手は普段どこで音楽を聴きますか？

    In the writer’s room, sitting on the bed.
  3. What do the writer and friends love doing at the park?

    4. 書き手と友達は公園で何をするのが大好きですか？

    They love playing soccer and talking about games.
  4. What does the writer hate?

    5. 書き手は何が嫌いですか？

    The writer hates staying at home all day and doing nothing.
  5. How does the writer feel during free time?

    6. 書き手は自由時間にどのように感じていますか？

    The writer feels happy when doing things they enjoy.

🎯Summary（まとめ）

  • like / love / hate + ing expresses preferences.
    like・love・hate ＋ ing で好みを表します。
  • It describes general activities, not one-time events.
    この形はふだんの活動について話すときに使います。
  • It is useful for talking about hobbies and free time.
    趣味や自由時間について話すときに便利です。
  • Keep sentences short and simple when expressing likes or dislikes.
    好き・嫌いを言うときは短くシンプルに言いましょう。
