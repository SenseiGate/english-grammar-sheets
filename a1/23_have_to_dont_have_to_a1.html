CEFR: A1

Lesson 23: Have to / Don't have to

📚 1. Grammar Explanation（文法の説明）

Have to shows obligation. It means something is necessary.

have to は義務を表し、「〜しなければならない」という意味です。

Don't have to means something is not necessary. It is not a prohibition.

don't have to は「〜する必要はない」という意味です。禁止ではありません。

Use have to + verb. With he / she / it, use has to and doesn't have to.

have to + 動詞の原形を使います。he / she / it のときは has to / doesn't have to になります。

SubjectFormExample
I / you / we / theyhave to + verbI have to work.
he / she / ithas to + verbShe has to study.
I / you / we / theydon't have to + verbYou don't have to come.
he / she / itdoesn't have to + verbHe doesn't have to cook.
you / we / theyDo … have to + verb?Do you have to go?
he / she / itDoes … have to + verb?Does she have to go?

✏️ 2. Short Example Sentences（短い例文）

  • I have to wake up early.
    私は早く起きなければなりません。
  • You don't have to bring anything.
    何も持ってくる必要はありません。
  • She has to study tonight.
    彼女は今夜勉強しなければなりません。
  • We don't have to hurry.
    急ぐ必要はありません。
  • They have to clean the room.
    彼らは部屋を掃除しなければなりません。
  • He doesn't have to cook today.
    彼は今日は料理をする必要はありません。
  • Do you have to go now?
    あなたは今行かなければなりませんか。

📝 3. Longer Example Sentences（長い例文）

  • I have to finish my homework before dinner.
    夕食の前に宿題を終えなければなりません。
  • You don't have to buy a ticket because it's free.
    無料なのでチケットを買う必要はありません。
  • She has to take the bus every morning.
    彼女は毎朝バスに乗らなければなりません。
  • We don't have to stay late after school.
    放課後遅くまで残る必要はありません。
  • He has to help his brother with homework.
    彼は弟の宿題を手伝わなければなりません。

📖 5. Short Reading Text（短い読解文）

My Busy Monday

My Monday is always very busy. I have to wake up early because I have a morning meeting at school. I have to bring my homework and my lunch. I don’t have to take the bus, because my dad drives me. After school, I have to go to piano class. I don’t have to cook dinner, but I have to help set the table. Even though it’s a busy day, I feel good when I finish everything.

忙しい月曜日

私の月曜日はいつもとても忙しいです。学校で朝の会があるので早く起きなければなりません。 宿題とお弁当を持っていかなければなりません。父が車で送ってくれるのでバスに乗る必要はありません。 放課後はピアノのレッスンに行かなければなりません。夕食を作る必要はありませんが、テーブルを準備しなければなりません。 忙しい日ですが、全部終わると気持ちがいいです。

❓ 6. Reading Comprehension Questions（読解問題）

1) Why does the writer have to wake up early?
なぜ早く起きなければなりませんか。

Because there is a morning meeting at school.

2) What does the writer have to bring?
何を持っていかなければなりませんか。

Homework and lunch.

3) Why doesn’t the writer have to take the bus?
なぜバスに乗る必要がありませんか。

Because the writer’s dad drives them.

4) What does the writer have to do after school?
放課後に何をしなければなりませんか。

Go to piano class.

5) What does the writer have to do at dinner time?
夕食の時間に何をしなければなりませんか。

Help set the table.

🎯 Summary（まとめ）

  • Have to shows obligation.
    have to は義務を表します。
  • Don’t have to means something is not necessary.
    don’t have to は必要がないことを表します。
  • Use Do you have to…? to ask about obligation.
    義務をたずねるときは Do you have to…? を使います。
  • Don’t have to is different from mustn’t.
    don’t have to と mustn’t は全く違う意味です。
